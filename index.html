Zuamawknak

CBANA Khawmpi ah Solo hlasak zuamawknak, choir zuamawknak le Nubu Lam zuamawknak tin zuamnak phun phun a um ding. Himi zuamawknak pawl ih hmat pek dan le tuanvo nei tu pawl le, contact pawl kan lo sim than leh ding. Solo le Choir hi mah kawhhran cio ah mah ten phuah in zuamawk ding a si. Cumi lawng silo in Bible zohlo zuamawknak tla a um ding. Bible zohlo zuamawkding bible cang cu Saam 37:1-40 a si ding. Tutum khawmpi ih thulu,"Na puan inn kauhter aw" timi thawn peh par in phuan ding a si pei.
